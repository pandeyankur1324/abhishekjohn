About Me

Actor

Welcome to my world of imagination and artistry! I am Abhishek John, an actor driven by the passion for storytelling and the boundless possibilities it offers. This website is your backstage pass into my creative universe, where I embrace the magic of the stage and screen.
From my very first encounter with the spotlight, I knew that this is my thing. Here, you'll find a collection of my performances, ranging from theater productions to on-screen portrayals. Each role I undertake is an opportunity for growth. Whether you're a fellow enthusiast of the performing arts, a casting director seeking the perfect fit, or someone who simply loves a good story, I invite you to join me on this creative expedition. Together, let us celebrate the magic of acting and the transformative power of storytelling.

Acting Workshop

For Aspiring Actors & creative souls.

Welcome to our Acting Workshop, where me & my fellow experienced & prodigious Acting Facilitator,Rumani Takkar. Share our Skills & experiences with you.we'll unlock your creative potential, refine your skills, and explore the art of authentic expression.

We have been Giving Acting workshops through DM STARS INDIA a casting and modelling agency,Moonlight Film & Television Institute,Take one school of Mass communication,Live wires Media Institute,Black Pearl Theater and Independently.

Whether you're a beginner, eager to explore the world of acting or an experienced performer seeking to sharpen your skills, our workshop offers an inspiring and supportive environment for all.

Together, we'll dive into the art of storytelling, character development, improvisation, and so much more. our aim is to help you unlock your full potential and build the confidence to shine on any stage,meet like-minded individuals, and become part of our passionate community of actors.

Let's shine on the stage together!
